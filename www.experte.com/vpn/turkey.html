Why Do You Need a VPN for Turkey?

Many Turkish websites and services are only available to those accessing them from Turkish IP addresses.

For public or state television content, this is because the usage or licensing rights only cover Turkey, and a user's location is determined on the basis of their IP address. Should you be in a country that isn't covered by the license, you won't be able to access the media or resource in question.

This is more commonly known as geoblocking and can be overcome with the help of a VPN since these can simulate a Turkish IP address. VPN users get a number of other benefits as well: Streaming services like Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, or Disney+ offer different content in Turkey, and subscriptions cost less than in Europe or North America.

This advantage extends to other downloadable offers like video games, audiobooks, or e-books. More details about how regional restrictions work, as well as tips for getting around them, can be found in our geoblocking guide.

Best Free VPN for Turkey

The five VPNs listed above are all either premium services or limit access to Turkish servers to paying subscribers. There are free offers among our Top 5 (ProtonVPN and hide.me), however, neither these nor any of the other free versions from our larger list allow non-subscribers to connect to Turkey.

As such, it isn't possible to use a free VPN to get a Turkish IP address. Most VPNs allow new users to try out their service for a limited period of time, but that's far from a permanent solution.

Should you need access to Turkish Internet content outside of Turkey, there's no way around a paid VPN.

Are VPNs Legal in Turkey?

Broadly speaking, VPN use is allowed in Turkey. However, Turkish authorities have begun to take steps against VPN providers and limit access to their websites. Should you want to use a VPN in Turkey, we recommend downloading and installing it prior to your arrival.

Conclusion

With any of our Top 5 VPNs for Turkey, you can surf the Internet as though you were sitting on the Bosporus. This provides several advantages: You can circumvent geoblocking and read local newspapers or stream Turkish films and series. Alternatively, you might save some money shopping online when using a Turkish IP address.

What is a VPN?
âŠ–

Virtual private networks create secure, encrypted connections between an end device and the Internet. This makes it possible to access the Internet without compromising your anonymity. VPNs offer other benefits too, like IP masking. This assigns you an IP address for the country that the server you've connected to is in. With this, you'll be able to access local content as though you were physically in that country.
