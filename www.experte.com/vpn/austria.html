Why Do You Need a VPN for Austria?

Borrowing an Austrian IP address offers a few advantages:

  • Access to Austrian media libraries
    Austrian media libraries, such as those of ORF or ATV can only offer content to those physically in Austria for licensing reasons. By using a VPN and connecting to an Austrian server, you'll be able to access these media libraries as though you were sitting in a Viennese cafe.

  • Explore streaming services' different content libraries
    You've probably heard of or even use Netflix, but did you know that the films and series available vary from country to country? If there's content that you'd like to watch but is unavailable where you're physically located, you can see if the Austrian versions have it.

  • Free Formula 1 streaming
    Formula 1 racing is rarely shown on free TV or even basic cable. Austrians are a bit luckier: Using ORF's TVTHEK, you can stream Formula 1, but only if you're in Austria, or on a VPN.

If you'd like to access Austria content, a VPN might be a worthwhile investment. However, not every VPN excels in each region, or even country.

How to Stream Austrian Content With a VPN

You've selected a VPN provider? There's not much left to do before you can circumvent Austrian region restrictions. Here, we'll show you how to do this using the top service from our assessment, NordVPN*:

Step 1: Install and open your provider's VPN client.

You can find and download all apps NordVPN offers in the user area of your account.

Step 2: Scroll through the country list for Austria, and click or tap on the server to establish a VPN connection.

Look for an Austrian server in the list or on the map.

Step 3: Now that you're connected to an Austrian server, you should be able to access streaming content or media libraries as though you were in the country.

Conclusion

If you're not in Austria, but want an uncomplicated (and legal) way to access Austrian content, a VPN service is the most practical solution. However, not every VPN can reliably bypass geoblocking on platforms such as ORF TVthek or ServusTV. The Top 5 VPN providers from our list will help bring you closer to Austria, even if you're on the other side of the world, by enabling access to region-restricted content.

You can find detailed reviews of individual providers in our EXPERTE.com VPN review.
