Remove Watermark From Photo: Automatically & Free

You have an unwanted watermark in one of your images? With the EXPERTE.com Image Cleaner, you can remove watermarks from your image automatically and without Photoshop knowledge.

No technical knowledge is required to use it. Just upload the image, mark the unwanted watermark and after a few seconds you will get the cleaned image.

How to Use

1. Upload the image you want to retouch automatically.

Photo with watermark

2. Select the watermark that should be removed automatically.

Selected watermark

3. As soon as you release the brush, the selected area is automatically removed. This can take up to 30 seconds, depending on the resolution of the image.

Photo without watermark

4. Download the cleaned image - in full resolution, of course.
